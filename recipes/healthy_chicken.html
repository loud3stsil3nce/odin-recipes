The Best Healthy Chicken Recipe

Searing hot garlic chicken with low fats!

Welcome to my favorite chicken recipe. This delicious garlic chicken recipe is my go-to, and has been, for years!

In order to make this recipe come to life, you should have a couple ingredients at hand. Make sure you have your chicken, seasoning (I use Badia Complete Seasoning), lemon juice, olive oil spray, and some plates!

Ingredients List

  • 8 oz chicken breast
  • 2 tsp seasoning powder of your choice
  • olive oil spray
  • lemon juice

How to Make the Best Chicken of Your Life

  1. Preheat your pan at med-high heat with some olive oil spray for a couple minutes beforehand. Gather all your materials. Make sure the chicken is placed in a sizeable bowl so that you can mix your seasoning well.
  2. Sprinkle your seasoning (around 2-3 tsp of Badia Complete Seasoning) onto the chicken as evenly as you can.
  3. Use your hands (sanitize them beforehand, obviously) to mix the seasoning well into the chicken. Add in a couple slaps for good measure, if you'd like.
  4. Place seasoned chicken onto already-warm pan. Should make fierce sheee sparkle sparkle noise once placed.
  5. Allow chicken to cook. Cover with lid if you please for more even distribution or whatever.
  6. After 30s-1min, flip chicken. Put a little bit of lemon juice/lime juice onto chicken as you please.
  7. Allow chicken to cook for a couple more minutes, or until it is as done as you'd like it.
  8. Say mama mia!, blow a kiss and enjoy your pollo frrr.
